Noor Miah

My creative practice involves focusing on mainstream film and cinema in relation to digital fashion film. I mainly focus on film language, such as image, aesthetics, narrative, time and time-space relationships. My practice then branches off to try and understand how these film concepts have been appropriated. This allows me to explore the relationship mainstream film and digital fashion film share. My work also explores the question of, ‘How does the viewer interact with film’, and other issues surrounding mainly conventional film and cinema.
