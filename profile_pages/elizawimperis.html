1.'Still from Performance' Charcoal on paper

3.'Reach' Carbon on paper

My practice stems from my fascination with walking. Derive-like wanderings are hinted at in my drawing practice and reflect the erratic routes my walks take me through the city. Using my training in classical ballet, and work in the circus world, I reimagine these city walks into abstract looping and crisscrossing lines. The aerial nature of my work reflects the unpredictable aspect of my meanderings. I suspend myself above the page, moving unhindered over the drawing plane representing my progress across the city â€“ the carbon in my hand traversing it, navigating it, allowing an exploration of the space in a multi perspective fashion.

Using randomly occurring patterns, the fall of scattered things both natural and the man-made, I also curate a symphony of the observed; this chance-like generated music accompanying the drawings.
