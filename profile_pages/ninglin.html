Ning Lin

A young artist from China, with an intriguing Oriental take on the world who loves to explores the depths of European culture using the medium of collage, street art and photographs. Their recent work included group photographs depicting different generations of new immigrants living in Europe and the impact on their lives of assimilating to local cultural norms. The photographs show the relationship between objects and people, highlighting the different relationships forged by their new surrounding and those of their ancestors.
