[p]
I am intrigued with the way image exists within different forms. Experimenting images to go through different processes, their transitional states offered a new form to the virtual information. Images went through being >>virtual>>physical>>compressed back to virtual>>rendered into a physical copy>>again and again. The heavily processed image started concealing the original image and reveal glitches itself experienced. The diminished visibility allowed traces of tactility that belongs to these digital mediums to be contained within. [/p]

[p]
I find the way 3d models enable images to embody a physicality intriguing. Experimenting with low quality images, programmes sculpt a new physicality to the captured subject. Mistakes invent new materiality to the familiar, thus compressing still images of a time-based installation into a frozen set. The fleeting images are compressed into a still model, afterwards to be rendered into image sequences to adopt the form of animation.
[/p]

[p]
The way moving images offer a sensorial experience will be the most intriguing thing between you and me. Experimenting with cuts, I will be leading you to see possibilities beyond the frame. Rapid arrangement of darkness and brightness will your eyes touch on the virtual images. Ultimately, all are aimed to make things to be seen another way.
[/p]
