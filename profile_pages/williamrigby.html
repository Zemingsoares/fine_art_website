Tweeted “think my Uber driver is on drugs”, within ~10 minutes getting a reply from Uber customer service saying “that doesn’t sound safe to us” --referring to me as “Winona” which had been my display name for the last ~10-15 months-- “please DM us your email address and rider number”, replied saying “jk he’s fine, I think”.

Yelled "CAN SOMEONE FUCKING HELP ME" internally as I dropped coins on floor in almost empty library then sat down opened my Macbook and looked at Gosha’s S/S collection, making a mental note to use more beige in my work. Was about to tweet “who the fuck is Elon Musk” but saved it as a draft, then scrolled through Kendall Jenners Twitter feed while persistently, mentally picturing Caitlyn Jenner speaking them aloud in a robotic, heavily affected, seemingly, “dystopian” manner. Put down my phone and took ‘Das Kapital’ out of my bag. After reading for 5-10 minutes, maybe 4 pages, that I re-read at least twice, sometimes up to 5 times before continuing with only a vague understanding and a stronger, lingering sense of exhaustion, I picked up my phone and tweeted “started reading ‘Das Kapital’ but stopped after ~5minutes because I literally don’t care”.
