Toni Dal

ONCE UPON A TIME A MANS HEAD FELL OFF. HE PLOPPED IT BACK ON AND EVERYTHING WAS ALRIGHT.

Toni Dal's aberrant observations of contemporary existence shine a light on our stark deprived reality and the frankly bizarre nature of humanity. With references to the social, cultural and political, to sex, drugs, war and wiretapped toasters, Dal takes precedence in the everyday and elevates the status of the mundane.
