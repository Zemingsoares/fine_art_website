How much impact does the media have on our lives? We are subjected to thousands of images every day that are saturated with hidden messages that sweep across the world and shape how our cultural, social and philosophical beliefs are formed.
The work I create hones in on these messages, examining the ideologies that are perpetuated by Hollywood cinema, tabloid newspapers, television and social media and how they control the way we see society and often dictate to us how to think and behave.
