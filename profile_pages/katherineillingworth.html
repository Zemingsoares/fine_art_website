KEJI

Walking.
Walking through my mind.
Walking through a street. Walking to a bus. Walking to a tube.
Ambling from a night tube.
What is happening?
People; a commodity,
Their ‘nothing’ moments,
My nothing moments.

Constrained…thoughts’, snapped.
Running,
if you run, do you not see?
Stand.
Standing in market of people, one for the artist to thieve.
Constrained by two eyes on their head.
Flowers of people littered and litter on the Asphalt (Benjamin 1974),
leaving a trace.

Time;

Walking away.
Running away.
ever to stand.

As Woolf (1930) described Life; deceived of her prey4,
Slip.
The scratch of a pencil,
The bleeding ink of one’s mind,
The money for a pencil,
The money for time,
The gift arguably given of a mind.

Is bleeding the right word?

What are they thinking?
What are you thinking?

Thoughts.
