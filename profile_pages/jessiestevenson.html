Jessie Mac Stevenson’s current work stems from her aim to expand the language of paint in contemporary practice. Jessie Mac seeks to explore the context and material approaches of paint, to create a unique encounter with the medium.
Through adopting various components including - synthetic material, a bright colour palette, and the dynamic orientation of the work in the space, Jessie Mac creates a feeling of uncertainty through her ‘skins’. By exploring the relationship between the observer, the material and the space, Jessie Mac aims to challenge the viewer’s preconceptions and experience the role of paint in a new and different way.
