Leah Caven

1.'Untitled' Digital video still

My practice consists of work with various medias incorporating sculptural elements with recorded audio and moving image. I am interested in the nuances of memory and recollection, physical and mental space and landscape, and suggestions of the sublime. Whilst recently focusing on how we view and position ourselves against those we consider as other through narrative recollection and the contrast and convergence between memory and internal landscape to the physical world.
