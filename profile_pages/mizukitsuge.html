Mizuki Tsuge makes installations which combine video, sound and photography to capture and express fleeting, intangible aspects of human experience such as emotions and memories. I am interested in working with the vulnerability inherent in human relationships; especially feelings of loss or emptiness which people tend to turn away from and struggle to comprehend. The original music compositions seek to release the emotions we hold within - loving, yearning, mourning, remembering… I continue to create an interaction of sound and image to find a way of imparting the transience of life.
