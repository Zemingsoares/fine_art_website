My aim through my practise yet emphasised in 3rd year is to push viewers and participants, (not always willing participants) of my work to their limits of comfort. Exploring all Physical, Emotional and Psychoanalytical forms of discomfort, I was able to gather knowledge of what truly puts us as humans on edge. Important areas throughout my recent practise include research into sexual organs, raw meat (the political views and the undesirable texture) and ‘The Camera,’ how differently an individual acts once they're aware that they're being filmed. Focusing on the use of raw meat for a while taught me a lot about why individuals have reservations with touching raw meat, yet I found the ‘unknown’ within the internet and not knowing who can see what we post a topic that caused a lot more discomfort within individuals.
