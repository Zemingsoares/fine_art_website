Interested in the construction of myth and their signifiers. Films, live events and other manifestations arise out of projects generally centred around the investigation of a particular myth or speculative fiction. A mixture of fictive and real world spaces and actions is used to create something at once true and untrue. Landscape figures heavily as both a backdrop and participant in the art works. Likes a good walk.
