Its dark outside its about half eight.
There is a man a couple of seats down with a folding bike resting on his shin. A woman sits texting opposite the tinny sound of something familiar playing in her ears.
There are so many places that i've been through countless times but never set foot in. They are so familiar, but unrecognisable. I pass the same orange square time and time again. Never seeing anyone in the window but the light is always on. I have no idea who you are or really where you are but you are always there, somewhere and nowhere. The places I pass become a clock a marker of how long till I get home. Is the train a clock to you? You are nowhere to me and I am nowhere to you but I have looked at your window more than I have looked at the woman opposite me on the train. I am nowhere but I am going home.
