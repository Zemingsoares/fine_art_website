Arnaud Meneroud

Considering the evolution of my previous work and experimentations throught different medium such as photography, video and installation, my practice always pursued the idea of exploring perception, reality and the human mind.
My first encounter with Virtual Relaity made me realise the power of this new medium.
Many of the "classical mediums" require a suspension of disbelief from the viewer in order to work.
In a near future this medium might have a consequent impact in the arts, but also in our everyday life, changing the face of the world and our relationship to reality.
My interest stands here. On the line where the user and the tool become one, breaking the rules that hold the distance between the viewer and the work.
