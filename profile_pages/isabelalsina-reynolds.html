Loki, arms heavy with the weight of Tesco’s shopping bags, calls out to Cerberus who can’t quite figure out which way round its new jumper goes, silly puppy.

My practice involves exploring the breached terrains of fiction vs non-fiction, artificial vs natural and myth vs reality. Working with video based installation to create fauxnonsensical and deceptive spaces that seek alternative truths, alternative dystopias, and alternative forms of human identity.
