Light is power, force and life.
It is energy that helps create the world and define our experiences.
Light may reveal and distort our perception of space and time.
It is not a dimension but a phenomenon subordinate to them.
Light becomes a creative tool that, if manipulated, can make things appear differently.
It can trick the mind, reinterpret reality and transform the environment.
Light modifies our perception of space by means of creating new sensorial experiences with the ephemeral vision.

Space and time may become distorted through light.
This altered perception determines how we see the world.
