Tom Coates uses sculpture, live art and installation to create work. With a mischievous attitude he gathers materials (many of them from the bin) coats them in bright colours and moulds strange forms before unleashing them on the world. He works in the hope of creating an unorthodox form of interaction between art and audience. Often using imagery from television, film, theatre and the domestic to augment his creations he often engages with and explores various forms of camp, to celebrate it and explore its potential to enrich the experience of the audience.
