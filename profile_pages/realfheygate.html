Realf Heygate takes artefacts through varied modes of reproduction, aiming to explore the archaeology of the object's image, rather than solely the object itself. The word ‘copy’ widely has derogatory status. This is despite roots as ‘copious’, implying abundance. In employing the copy, Realf approaches the original as the source of a long lineage. Traditional notions of authenticity are then probed in tracing migrations through mediums.
