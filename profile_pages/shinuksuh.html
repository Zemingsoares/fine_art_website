1. Insignificant Tensions In My Life-1
2. Insignificant Tensions In My Life-2

I have awoken to the fact that my life is full of insignificant tensions, and I believe that subconscious memories of mine have been dominated by these tensions. My practice describes that situation. Various materials and items that I have picked for my artwork create some awkward balance and harmony. I have analysed each 'insignificant tension' that I feel from the items, and tried to suggest a further tension through their placings in my work.
