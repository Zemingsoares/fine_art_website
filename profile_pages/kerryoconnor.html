ENTER STAGE RIGHT.

A spotlight hits.
I make my presence known.

SHOOT.
Out with the words.
Crunch and crack.
Chewing down the unbreakable
Linguistics.
That ruptures the very being that creates.
You.
I.


Here I am.
Glowing flesh. Melting the eyes in awe.
Crippling. It is shot. A target.
Weaker. Much easier to define.

You are a star baby.
