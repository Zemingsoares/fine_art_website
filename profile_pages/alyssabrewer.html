3.‘Memory Box’ 2017

Looking into the topics of memory and nostalgia, my work is story based. Using objects, and developing them into texts, my work is created with the intentions of recreating feelings of nostalgia for it’s audience. By exploring memory through various senses, such as sight, sound and smell, I aim to appeal to a wider audience in the hope to create comfort, and increase their chance of retrieving memories.
