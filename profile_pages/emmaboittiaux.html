Emma Boittiaux

I want to transform material into skin. Silicone is the representation of skin on a body, an invention of a new perception of it, whereas the resin covers the image and creates a blur between photography and sculpture. The new image becomes torn between a cloudy sky and a frozen lake. The wax is a new layer that reveals the faces of people underneath it.
