Victoria Rivers

Victoria is a performance artist who uses variant platforms such as writing/editing, staging and filming as vessels to question societal conceptions of nationalism and its embedded existence in sense perception. Her upbringing in Bulgaria serves as the output for her cultural influences which are echoed throughout her work, most consistently in her video footage. The performances are driven by borrowed texts from playwrights such as Dennis Kelly and Sarah Kane as well as her own re-presentation of the text and characters. In her performances Victoria implicates the viewer to become part of the performance with the use of staging and speech. This breaks the threshold between the audience and the stage and opens up a platform which can question and challenge social constructs. Additionally, Victoria is a working actor and in her developing work she portrays her interests of different acting techniques such as Method acting, Meisner, and Stanislavsky's teachings.
