Crispy Crackolates
You will need:
1 oz sugar
1 0z butter
1 oz cocoa
1 tablespoon golden syrup
1 oz cornflakes

Scales
Saucepan
Metal tablespoon
Large dish.

1. Put 1 oz each of sugar, butter and cocoa into the pan.
2. Add 1 tablespoon golden syrup (this will be easier if you warm the tablespoon in hot water first).
3. Turn on the ring and leave it on low.
4. Put the pan over the heat and melt the ingredients slowly, taking great care that they do not boil.
5. When melted, remove from the gas and cool slightly.
6. Add 1 oz cornflakes and stir in with the metal spoon.
7. When the cornflakes are thoroughly coated with the mixture, spoon into small piles on the dish and leave to set.
