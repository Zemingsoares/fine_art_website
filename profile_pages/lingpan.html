1.'TREELOGY' Immersive Theatre

My mind blows up the word Gammadion
I have no place for it left in my heart
It was a pool a peculair pool of sight in the near indexses of our forest.
We would go carving, in the woodstroken dawn.

THer eis a poool
THere is a pool of chaose
Around, encloses us.

We are afraid, yes

Ganli glim climbs ups on us all
Day long...

Sicouetti, you sinewed soul.

I am writing for you.
